Citizens’ Collective for Safe Cyberspace is composed of civil society members seeking safe cyberspace for all. The core interests of the Collective are to ensure that online platforms are inclusive and safe for women, gender and social minorities to freely express their voice without fear of harassment and bullying. The goal of the Collective is to build and strengthen systems directed to ensuring safety in online spaces. We are a growing Collective borne out of the urgent need to change policies and behaviours online following a spate of virulent bullying and harassment enabled through cyberspace.
